Hardware Design

Our hardware contains the RP2040, the VGA wiring, a microphone connection, and GPIO wirings to the three buttons used. The VGA is tied to five assigned GPIO pins of the RP2040 for the five inputs, and also tied to the ground. The R, G, and B inputs are passed over a 330Ohm resistor. The microphone is powered by the RP2040 and tied to its ground, with its data being passed to the DMA of the RP2040 via the ADC channel. We also have the UART serial connection in our circuit from a test version of our game that used key inputs, but that is no longer applied in our final result.
